Our Partner

international

Our literacy materials are available in 30 languages, including the major international languages of English, French, Dutch, Spanish, Portugese and Arabic. We love to partner with like-minded organisations around the world towards the common mission of literacy for all. Reach out to us today to explore collaboration.

Governments

DEVI Sansthan believes in partnering with governments to help catalyse educational transformation. We provide groundbreaking literacy materials, impactful teacher training, and knowledge partnership every step of the way. Explore some of our brightest government partnerships so far below, and get in touch with us today to explore collaboration.

Corporate

Literacy is the world’s best investment, yielding a tremendous social return. DEVI Sansthan is privileged to be partnering with some of India’s finest CSR foundations to bring literacy to thousands of children and adults. If you want to make a contribution, please donate today.
